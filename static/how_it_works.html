How It Works

The Science of AI Artistry

The Process

  1. Capture: Use your webcam to take a photo.
  2. Select: Choose an artistic style (e.g., Van Gogh or Picasso).
  3. Transform: Our AI, powered by neural networks, blends your photo’s content with the chosen style.
  4. Display: See your unique artwork instantly!

The Technology

We use a pre-trained neural network from TensorFlow Hub, fine-tuned for style transfer. Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) analyze your photo’s structure and the style image’s textures, merging them into a new masterpiece.

This demo runs on a Flask server with Python, showcasing real-time AI processing for an engaging exhibition experience.

About ArtisticAI

ArtisticAI is a cutting-edge neural style transfer application that transforms your ordinary photos into extraordinary art in the style of famous painters.
